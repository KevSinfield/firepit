Our story

It started with a garden oven

The Fire Kitchen started with one obsession: pizza cooked properly, stone-baked over a live flame. After years of messing about with dough at home and feeding friends from a tiny garden oven, we built a mobile pizzeria and took it out on the road.

Every dough is slow-proved for 48 hours, topped with the best we can get our hands on, and cooked in front of you in about 90 seconds. No shortcuts, no fuss, just proper pizza cooked over fire.

Book the truck for your event

Weddings, birthdays, work dos, festivals: we bring the oven and feed your crowd fresh pizza all night. And it's not only pizza. We also do BBQs, paella and buffets. Send us the date and rough numbers and we'll put a quote together.
