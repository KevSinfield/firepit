Our story

It started with a garden oven

Placeholder: Craig and Amy's real story goes here

The Fire Kitchen started with one obsession: pizza cooked properly, over real wood flame. After years of messing about with dough at home and feeding friends from a tiny garden oven, we built a mobile pizzeria and took it out on the road.

Every dough is slow-proved for 48 hours, topped with the best we can get our hands on, and cooked in front of you in about 90 seconds. No shortcuts, no fuss, just proper pizza cooked over fire.

Book the truck for your event

Weddings, birthdays, work dos, festivals: we bring the oven, the dough and the fire, and feed your crowd fresh pizza all night. Send us the date and rough numbers and we'll put a quote together.
